His fascination with antiquity began at the age of ten through the collection and study of ancient coins, an interest that quickly grew into a lasting engagement with classical history and literature.

His particular passions lie in epic and its afterlives: from Homer and Vergil to James Joyce, tracing how ancient narratives continue to shape and resurface in modern literature.
